About

Makeup by Shae offers a wide range of makeup and hair styling services. The benefit of hiring a freelance makeup artist is that you can get ready for your special event in the comfort of your own home. Shae, the makeup artist, will meet you anywhere you'd like within the Lower Mainland. You can book individually or, if you prefer, as a group. Since graduating from the prestige Blanche MacDonald makeup school, Shae has been doing freelance makeup for many different types of events and would be happy to work with you for yours. Please contact Shae with the day and a description of your event and she will email you back as soon as possible with her availability and a quote.

About Shae

I have always been a super creative person. Makeup has allowed me to harness this creativity and pick up on people’s individual, unique looks and emphasize them in order to make them look their best.

I graduated from the Blanch Macdonald's renowned Freelance Makeup Artistry program in 2017. As Blanche is the #1 Makeup school in Canada, I had access to some of the best teachers and resources in the industry. This program provided me with training not only in makeup fundamentals, bridal makeup, airbrushing techniques fashion and editorial makeup, but also basic and bridal hairstyling.

I’ve proudly grown up in the Fraser Valley my whole life and love everything that B.C has to offer. Outside of makeup, I have a huge passion for music and enjoy playing some classic Pink Floyd on my guitar. I have always loved photography and capturing the perfect image in a unique light. Any extra free time I have is spent taking walks with my adorable puppy Koda.
