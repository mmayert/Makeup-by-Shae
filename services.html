Event Makeup

This can be anything from a photo shoot to a Christmas work party. I can provide you with any look that you'd like wether it be a light everyday look to a sexy smoky eye night out look. Feel free to bring in photos of the kind of look you'd like to go for, or, leave the artistry up to me and I'll be sure to pick the best look for you! An event makeup look includes: face makeup (including blush, contouring and highlighting), eye makeup (shadow, liner and mascara), brows and lips. Whatever your special occasion, you will look your best with a makeup look by Shae!


Extras available:

Fake eye lashes (+10$) or airbrush makeup (+40$).

Grad Makeup

There's nothing more exciting for a young girl than graduating! Pamper the grad with a makeover for grad photo's, their ceremony or prom! We can craft a unique look to fit your gown or feel free to bring in any photos you like for inspiration! We can even do group makeup bookings so you and your friends can get ready together. Grad makeup includes: face makeup (including blush, contouring and highlighting), eye makeup (shadow, line and mascara), brows and lips. Trial runs are available upon request. I'll make sure that you look absolutely fantastic on this exciting day.


Extras available:

Fake eye lashes (+10$) or airbrush makeup (+40$).

Bridal Makeup

This is the biggest day in a woman's life! You want to make sure that you look back on pictures and looked your best. I'll be sure that you're photo ready, and more importantly radiantly stunning as you walk down the aisle on your special day. Not only can I do the bride's makeup, I can also do all the brides maids' and mother of the bride's/groom's makeup! And don't forget some lipgloss for the flower girl aswell! Grad makeup includes: face makeup (including blush, contouring and highlighting), eye makeup (shadow, line and mascara), brows and lips. Trial runs are available upon request. We'll work together in creating a perfect look for you and your girls on this special day.


Extras available:

Fake eye lashes (+10$) or airbrush makeup (+40$).
